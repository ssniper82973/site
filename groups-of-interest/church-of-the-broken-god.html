Church of the Broken God, abbreviated and also known as CBG, is a neutral-hostile techno-religious cult and Group of Interest.

Introduction

The Church of the Broken God is a widespread techno-religious cult that worships Mekhane, a deity believed to have been a great mechanical being shattered in ancient times. Its followers see the organic body as impure and weak, striving instead for perfection through mechanical augmentation and cybernetic enhancement. They believe that by gathering and reconstructing the scattered fragments of their god, often taking the form of anomalous machinery, they can restore Mekhane to wholeness and bring about a new age of order and perfection. Often in opposition to other anomalous groups, particularly the Sarkic cults who revere flesh and mutation, the Church views its mission as both sacred and inevitable.

Leadership Structure

CBG operates under a hierarchical structure led by their leader, legitgamer678. Under the leader there are two known Second in Commands, Lilyy_NightShade and alexsimms2539. No other information regarding their structure or chain of command is known at this time.
